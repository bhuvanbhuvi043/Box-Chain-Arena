## Project

**Box Chain Arena**  
Official Version 1 - Public Testing Build

Box Chain Arena is an original web-based strategy game prototype created by Bhuvaneshwaran R. The game is inspired by the classic childhood paper game of dots and boxes, and has been independently designed and developed with custom duel arenas, guided learning, Firebase-powered multiplayer, player profiles, virtual Entry Gold, rewards, progression systems, AI opponents, and online challenge features.

Live Demo:  
https://bhuvanbhuvi043.github.io/Box-Chain-Arena/
