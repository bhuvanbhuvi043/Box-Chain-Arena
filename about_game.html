About Box Chain Arena

Kingdom strategy game

Box Chain Arena is a dots-and-boxes strategy game with shaped stages, AI rivals, offline two-player games, private online rooms, player challenges, and virtual progression rewards.

How to Play

Draw a line between adjacent dots. Completing a box claims it for your kingdom and gives another turn. Capture the majority of boxes to win.

Game Modes

  • Solo stages against AI opponents.
  • Offline two-player matches on one device.
  • Registered online rooms and player challenges.
  • Teaching Demo and strategy Guide.
