Box Chain Arena Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Box Chain Arena is developed by Bhuvi Digital Works. Guest play stays on the device and does not create an anonymous Firebase account. Registered online play uses Firebase Authentication, App Check, Cloud Functions, and Realtime Database.

Information We Process

  • Private account data: email, nickname reservation, Challenge Points, point reservations, block list, limited progress totals, and settlement ledger entries.
  • Limited public profile: nickname, online status, last-seen time, wins, losses, and match count.
  • Online play: participant IDs, names, arena, legal moves, scores, Challenge Points, Victory Bonus, timestamps, invites, reports, and verified match results.
  • Support: information you choose to email, such as device details, screenshots, nickname, or room code.

Solo Gold, Diamonds, shop inventory, and detailed solo-stage saves remain on the device.

How We Use Data

We use this information to provide login, online rooms, server-validated moves, one-time match settlement, presence, reports, blocks, fraud prevention, account deletion, and support. We do not sell personal data.

Sharing and Retention

Google Firebase processes data to operate authentication and online services. Challenge Points and all rewards are virtual only and have no cash, withdrawal, resale, cryptocurrency, or real-world prize value.

When an account is deleted, associated profiles, nickname, presence, invites, rooms, matches, blocks, points, and account indexes are deleted. Reports may remain only after account identifiers are anonymized for abuse prevention. Provider security logs may remain for limited security or legal periods.

Your Choices

Use Menu, Profile, Delete Account inside the game, or follow the public Account Deletion instructions.
