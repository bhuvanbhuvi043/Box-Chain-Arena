Delete Your Box Chain Arena Account

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Registered players can delete their account and associated online data from inside the game or request deletion by email.

Delete in the App

  1. Open Box Chain Arena.
  2. Choose Menu, then Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm.
  4. If asked, log out, log in again, and repeat deletion. A login within the previous hour is required for security.

Request Deletion Without the App

Email the registered email address and nickname with the subject "Box Chain Arena Account Deletion Request."

Request Account Deletion

Data Deleted

Deletion covers Firebase Authentication, private and public profiles, nickname reservation, presence, invites, rooms, match records, blocks, Challenge Points, limited cloud progress totals, and account indexes.

Solo Gold, Diamonds, inventory, and detailed stage progress are device-local and are removed by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. Reports may be retained only after account identifiers are anonymized for abuse prevention. Provider security logs may remain for limited security or legal periods.

Guest Players

Guest play does not create a Firebase account. Clear app storage or uninstall the app to remove guest data from the device.
