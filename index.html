Privacy & Data

Box Chain Arena uses Firebase for login, online rooms, player presence, invites, match records, saved progress, and virtual rewards. Gold, stars, diamonds, Entry Gold, and Reward Pots are virtual game rewards only and have no real money value.

Registered play stores email, nickname, online status, match stats, saved progress, and virtual rewards. Guest play can use anonymous Firebase identity for room-code testing. You can request or perform account deletion from Player Profile.
